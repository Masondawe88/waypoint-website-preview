Radius is 0 everywhere — architecture, not upholstery. The pill radius exists only for progress dots and indicators. The single shadow token is reserved for elements that physically float (sticky bars, image stacks); everything else earns elevation through whitespace.

03 · Actions

Two action languages, strictly zoned. Editorial links are the default everywhere the site is telling a story. Buttons exist only inside forms and at final conversion moments — never in narrative sections. One accent colour, one verb style, no exceptions.

.wp-action — editorial links (narrative default)
Explore yacht charter Plan a charter View the journey Read more in the journal.
Begin a journey
.wp-btn — forms & conversion only

States: hover shifts to --wp-accent-soft; focus is the global 1.5px accent outline at 4px offset; .is-disabled drops to stone/muted; .is-loading hides the label behind a single light sweep — no spinners. The floating CTA .wp-float-cta is a glass bar reserved for mobile "Begin a journey" persistence.

09 · Motion

One curve — cubic-bezier(.22, 1, .36, 1) — governs everything. Three durations: 900ms media, 700ms reveals, 300ms micro. World identities persist from Phase Two: AIR moves vertically, SEA horizontally, STAY spatially. Parallax never exceeds 10% differential. Reduced motion collapses everything to instant, visible states.

.wp-reveal — fade & settle

Arrives 14px below, settles over 700ms.

.wp-img-reveal — clip reveal
.wp-text-reveal — line mask

Seamless is engineered.

Page transitions reuse the Phase Two veil (directional sweep in --wp-bg-dark). Hover is opacity + hairline + ≤2.5% media scale — never colour flashes or lifts. Video crossfades at 900ms with poster-first loading. Scroll behaviour: reveals trigger at 12% visibility with −6% root margin, once only, top-to-bottom order.
