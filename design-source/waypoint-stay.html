The rhythm of a stay

One day, unscheduled.

Early
Coffee before the house wakes.
Bare feet on stone. A door left open to the breeze.
Morning
The destination begins outside the door.
Towels after the ocean. Wet swimwear drying on the rail.
Midday
Lunch returns to the table.
Produce being prepared. A table set slowly, without occasion.
Afternoon
The residence becomes quiet again.
A book beside a window. Light moving across timber.
Evening
Fire, water, wine or sunset.
A fire being lit. The day settling into the landscape.
Night
Nothing scheduled.
Children asleep. The house holding its quiet.
