# WAYPOINT & Co. — Real-Asset Photography Requests · v1.4
Standard for every asset: RAW+JPEG, minimum 4000px long edge (heroes 6000px preferred),
written usage rights (web, social, print collateral; territory worldwide; term ≥3 years),
landscape hero **and** portrait alternate for every key shot, mobile 16:10 safe-centre crop
respected (subject within central 70%), natural light preferred, no heavy grading.
Unsuitable throughout: heavy HDR, fisheye interiors, other guests visible, branding of third
parties, out-of-date configurations, anything contradicting listed capacity/specs.
