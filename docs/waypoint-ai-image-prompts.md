# WAYPOINT & Co. — AI Prompt Template Library + First Editorial Batch · v1.4
No brand names, no photographer names, no living-artist imitation. Visual characteristics only.
Global negative baseline (append to every prompt): *oversaturated sky, HDR, lens flare, posed
models, champagne, logos, text, watermarks, distorted machinery, warped anatomy, obvious
symmetry, artificial beach perfection.*

## Templates
Each: Subject · Location · Time · Camera · Lens · Composition · Light · Colour · People · Texture · Exclusions · Ratio.

**T1 Destination hero** — Broad landform of the destination; from elevation or water; first or last light; distant camera, slight aerial; medium tele compression; layered depth with negative space; directional low sun; restrained warm grade; no people or one distant figure; atmospheric haze; exclude landmarks implying private access; 21:9.
**T2 Destination landscape** — A single landscape idea (river mouth, ridge, reef edge); ground or low-drone; golden hour or soft overcast; natural-eye 35–50mm character; rule-of-thirds with breathing room; ambient light only; honest colour; no people; visible surface texture; exclude resort furniture; 3:2.
**T3 Journey transition** — The moment between modes (window to water, jetty to deck, car to strip); passenger point of view; morning; handheld feel; foreground frame element; available light; neutral-warm; hands or partial presence only; material detail in foreground; exclude faces; 16:9.
**T4 Journal cover** — One editorial idea from the story; observational distance; time matching the story; documentary 35mm character; off-centre subject; natural light; muted palette; unposed distant presence allowed; place texture; exclude anything staged; 3:2.
**T5 Detail texture** — Close material study (rope, linen, timber, chart paper, water surface); macro-to-close; window or low sun; shallow depth; fills frame with texture; raking light; warm neutral; hands permitted mid-task; tactile grain; exclude jewellery/branding; 4:5.
**T6 Future world** — Distant, deliberately unspecific geography; high altitude or far offshore; dusk haze; long lens; minimal composition, heavy negative space; low-contrast light; desaturated warmth; no people; atmospheric softness; exclude recognisable landmarks; 4:3.
**T7 Social sharing** — Simplified single-subject frame readable at small size; centred-weighted; strong early/late light; clean background; house palette warmth; no people; bold texture; exclude fine detail that dies at 630px; 1200×630.
**T8 Editorial human moment** — One person mid-action (casting off, walking a jetty, reading a chart); observational, never to camera; morning; 50mm character; subject small within place; natural light; honest tones; back or profile only; environmental texture; exclude posing and eye contact; 3:2.
**T9 AIR atmosphere** — Generic unmarked airframe in landscape context (strip, apron, taxi, climb-out); ground level or following; dawn; readable silhouette, accurate proportions; aircraft subordinate to place; low warm sun; no livery or registration; heat-haze or prop blur; exclude cabin interiors and invented types; 16:9.
**T10 SEA atmosphere** — Water itself, or an unidentifiable vessel small in frame; low aerial or waterline; golden hour; wake lines and tidal texture as subject; directional light on water; green-blue restraint; no branding; surface detail; exclude deck layouts; 16:9.
**T11 STAY atmosphere** — Believable architecture within landscape, generic not replicating any listed residence; exterior threshold distance; dusk with interior glow; wide but undistorted; structure integrated in vegetation/landform; mixed ambient; natural material palette; no people; timber/stone texture; exclude implausible cantilevers and any resemblance to The Cowries, JASMINE, ONE W, Mountain Majesty; 16:9.
